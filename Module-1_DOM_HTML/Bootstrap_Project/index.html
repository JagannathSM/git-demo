Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as a Bidhohi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philoshopher of Bangladesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writing deal with the revolution of that period of the British government. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kind of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity, and revolution. He opposed all form of bigotry and fundamentalism, including religious, cast-based and gender-based. Throughout his career, Nazrul wrote short stories, novels, and essays but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Narzul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songa (many recorded on HMV and gramophone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.
